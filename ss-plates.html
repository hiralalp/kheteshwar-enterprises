About Stainless Steel Plates

Stainless steel plates are thick flat-rolled products with thickness typically ranging from 6mm to 100mm and above. They are manufactured using hot rolling or forging processes and are designed for heavy-duty structural and pressure vessel applications. Plates offer superior strength and are ideal for applications requiring high load-bearing capacity.

Plate Categories

  • Hot Rolled Plates: Economical, suitable for welded structures
  • Cold Rolled Plates: Better surface finish and dimensional accuracy
  • Clad Plates: Stainless steel bonded to carbon steel
  • Polished Plates: Mirror or brushed finish for aesthetic use
  • Pressure Vessel Quality: For boilers and pressure vessels
  • Structural Plates: For bridges, buildings, and platforms
  • Wear Resistant: High hardness for abrasive environments
  • Heat Resistant: For high temperature applications

Quality Testing

All plates undergo stringent quality tests including ultrasonic testing (UT), radiographic testing (RT), magnetic particle inspection (MPI), chemical analysis, mechanical property testing, and dimensional verification. We provide test certificates complying with EN 10204 3.1 or 3.2 standards as required.

Value Added Services

We offer comprehensive value-added services including custom cutting to size, edge beveling for welding preparation, surface grinding, shot blasting, heat treatment, and protective coating application. Our in-house facilities ensure quick turnaround and quality control.
