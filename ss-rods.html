About Stainless Steel Rods

Stainless steel rods are solid bars manufactured in round, square, hexagonal, and flat shapes through hot rolling, cold drawing, or forging processes. They are essential raw materials for manufacturing fasteners, shafts, valves, fittings, and precision-machined components across various industries. Our rods combine excellent corrosion resistance with superior mechanical properties.

Types & Shapes

  • Round Bars: Most common, 3mm to 300mm diameter
  • Square Bars: For construction and fabrication work
  • Hexagonal Bars: Ideal for bolts and fastener production
  • Flat Bars: Wide range of widths and thicknesses
  • Bright Bars: Cold drawn, precision ground finish
  • Black Bars: Hot rolled, economical option
  • Peeled Bars: Centerless ground for accuracy
  • Polished Bars: Mirror finish for decorative use

Material Properties

Our stainless steel rods exhibit excellent tensile strength, yield strength, elongation, hardness, and impact resistance. Grade 303 offers superior machinability for automatic screw machine operations, while 304 and 316 grades provide better corrosion resistance. Specialized grades like 410, 420, and 440C offer higher hardness for cutting tools and bearings.

Industry Applications

SS rods find extensive use in fastener manufacturing (bolts, nuts, screws), shaft production, valve stems, pump components, marine hardware, chemical equipment, food processing machinery, pharmaceutical equipment, precision instruments, automotive parts, and general machining applications. We supply to manufacturers across diverse industries.
