About Stainless Steel Circles

Stainless steel circles are precision-cut round discs manufactured from high-quality stainless steel sheets and coils. They are produced using advanced laser cutting or CNC punching technology ensuring accurate dimensions and smooth edges. Available in diameters from 50mm to 1000mm, these circles are essential raw materials for manufacturing various circular products.

Manufacturing Process

  • Laser Cutting: Precise cuts with minimal edge distortion
  • CNC Punching: High-speed production for standard sizes
  • Shearing: Cost-effective for thicker materials
  • Water Jet: For heat-sensitive applications
  • Edge Finishing: Deburred and polished edges
  • Flatness Control: Minimal warpage or distortion
  • Surface Protection: PVC film for scratch prevention
  • Quality Inspection: 100% dimensional verification

Applications & Uses

SS circles are widely used in manufacturing cookware (pressure cookers, pans, tawa), utensils, sinks, lighting fixtures, automotive components, kitchen equipment, signage, decorative items, and industrial machinery parts. The circles serve as blanks for deep drawing, spinning, and stamping operations.
