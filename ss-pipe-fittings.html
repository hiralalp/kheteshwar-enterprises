About SS Pipe Fittings

Stainless steel pipe fittings are essential components used to connect, control flow, and change direction in piping systems. They include elbows, tees, reducers, couplings, caps, flanges, and unions. These fittings ensure leak-proof connections and are critical for maintaining system integrity in various industrial applications.

Types of Pipe Fittings

  • Elbows: 45°, 90°, 180° for directional changes
  • Tees: Equal and reducing tees for branching
  • Reducers: Concentric and eccentric for size changes
  • Couplings: Full and half couplings for connections
  • Flanges: Slip-on, weld neck, blind, lap joint
  • Caps: End caps for terminating pipe runs
  • Unions: For easy disassembly and maintenance
  • Nipples: Short pipe sections for connections

Key Advantages

Our stainless steel pipe fittings offer superior corrosion resistance, high pressure and temperature ratings, ease of installation, long service life, and compatibility with various piping systems. They are manufactured to precise dimensions ensuring perfect fit and leak-free performance.

Manufacturing Standards

All our pipe fittings are manufactured according to ANSI/ASME B16.9 (butt weld fittings), ASME B16.11 (socket weld and threaded fittings), and ASME B16.5 (flanges) standards. We provide mill test certificates and complete material traceability for all products.
